## Hours of operation

<p><strong>Behavioral Health Urgent Care (BHUC)</strong> hours:</p>
<ul><li><strong>Walk-in urgent care:</strong> Monday–Friday 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM; Saturday–Sunday 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM (Central Time).</li><li><strong>Telehealth visits:</strong> Daily 7:00 AM – 10:00 PM.</li><li><strong>Crisis support (988):</strong> 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.</li></ul>
<p>We are closed on major U.S. holidays; crisis support via 988 is always available.</p>


## Location and parking

<p>BHUC is located at <strong>1420 Riverside Drive, Suite 200, Austin, TX 78704</strong>.</p>
<p>We are on the second floor; take the elevator to Suite 200. <strong>Free on-site parking</strong> is available in the lot behind the building, with additional street parking on Riverside Drive. The entrance is wheelchair accessible.</p>


## Contact us

<p><strong>Main line:</strong> (512) 555-0100 (Mon–Fri 8 AM–8 PM, Sat–Sun 10 AM–6 PM).</p>
<p><strong>Care team messages:</strong> use the secure messaging in your patient portal.</p>
<p><strong>In crisis?</strong> Call or text <strong>988</strong> anytime. If this is a life-threatening emergency, call 911.</p>


## Insurance we accept

<p>BHUC accepts most major plans, including:</p>
<ul><li>Blue Shield</li><li>Aetna</li><li>UnitedHealthcare</li><li>Cigna</li><li>Texas Medicaid (STAR)</li><li>Medicare</li></ul>
<p>Don&#39;t see your plan? Contact the front desk at (512) 555-0100 — we can verify coverage and discuss options. Self-pay and sliding-scale pricing are also available.</p>


## Self-pay and financial assistance

<p>If you&#39;re uninsured or prefer to pay directly, BHUC offers <strong>self-pay pricing with a sliding scale</strong> based on income. A <strong>financial counselor</strong> is available to review estimated costs and payment plans before your visit — ask the front desk or request a counselor from your patient portal (Coverage screen).</p>


## Services we offer

<p>BHUC provides behavioral-health urgent care, including:</p>
<ul><li>Crisis stabilization and safety planning</li><li>Suicide-risk and mental-health screening (C-SSRS, PHQ-9, GAD-7)</li><li>Medication management</li><li>Brief therapy and counseling</li><li>Substance use disorder (SUD) support — protected under 42 CFR Part 2</li><li>Prior-authorization and coverage help</li><li>Care coordination, referrals, and post-visit follow-up</li></ul>


## What to bring to your visit

<p>Please bring:</p>
<ul><li>A photo ID</li><li>Your insurance card (if you have one)</li><li>A list of current medications and doses</li><li>An emergency contact&#39;s name and phone number</li></ul>
<p>New patients can save time by completing registration and consent in the patient portal before arriving.</p>


## How to register and start care

<p>You can start care two ways:</p>
<ol><li><strong>Online:</strong> Create an account in the BHUC patient portal, complete registration and consent (including the separate 42 CFR Part 2 consent for substance-use records), and complete your intake screening.</li><li><strong>In person:</strong> Walk in during urgent-care hours and register at the front desk.</li></ol>
<p>You control each consent independently and can decline any of them.</p>


## Telehealth visits

<p>BHUC offers <strong>telehealth visits daily 7:00 AM – 10:00 PM</strong>. After booking, a secure video link appears on your Appointments screen and becomes active about 15 minutes before your visit. You&#39;ll need a phone, tablet, or computer with a camera and a private space. Telehealth is not for emergencies — in a crisis, call or text 988.</p>


## Crisis and emergency support

<p>If you or someone else is in crisis, help is available 24/7:</p>
<ul><li><strong>Call or text 988</strong> — the Suicide &amp; Crisis Lifeline (free, confidential, 24/7).</li><li><strong>Call 911</strong> for a life-threatening emergency.</li></ul>
<p>BHUC&#39;s front-door assistant will always connect you to 988 and a live coordinator if it detects a crisis, day or night.</p>


## Accessibility and language services

<p>BHUC&#39;s facility is wheelchair accessible. We offer <strong>interpreter services</strong> in many languages and <strong>ASL</strong> on request at no cost. Let the front desk or your care team know your needs when you register.</p>
